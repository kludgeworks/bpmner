# Fulfilment with optional extras

When an order reaches the packing station, two optional add-on services may
run before the package leaves the warehouse. Each is evaluated independently
against the order, and any subset of the add-ons can fire on the same
package — gift wrap and the promotional insert are not mutually exclusive
and a customer with both eligible flags gets both.

If the customer requested gift wrap at checkout, the gift-wrap station
applies the seasonal wrapping and attaches the personalised card the
customer composed at the time of purchase. Independently, if the order
qualifies for the current promotional insert — its weight, dimensions, and
shipping destination all permit, and the customer has not opted out of
marketing material — the insert station tucks the flyer into the package
alongside the goods.

Either, both, or neither of these add-ons may apply on any given order.
When neither condition is true, the package proceeds straight from the
packing station to labelling without delay. When one is true, only that
station does its work. When both are true, the two stations run
concurrently — the staff at each station do not wait for the other — and
the package proceeds to labelling once whichever stations were activated
have completed their work.

Once the add-ons have settled, the package is labelled with the carrier's
shipping label, handed to the carrier driver at the dock, and the order's
status is updated to "shipped" in the order-management system.
