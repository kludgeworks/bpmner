Make toast for breakfast:

1. Start in the kitchen.
2. Check whether there is bread available.
3. If there is no bread, stop the process and report that toast cannot be made.
4. If bread is available, take two slices of bread.
5. Put the bread slices into the toaster.
6. Set the toaster to a medium browning level.
7. Start the toaster.
8. Wait until the toaster finishes.
9. Remove the toast from the toaster carefully.
10. Check whether the toast is burned.
11. If the toast is burned, discard it and restart the process with new bread if more bread is available.
12. If the toast is not burned, put the toast on a plate.
13. Decide whether butter is desired.
14. If butter is desired, spread butter on the toast.
15. Serve the toast.
16. End the process.
